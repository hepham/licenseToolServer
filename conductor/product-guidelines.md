# Product Guidelines

## Product Identity

- **Name:** LicenseToolServer
- **Tagline:** Simple license management for your tools

## Communication Tone

- **Style:** Friendly & Approachable
- Use simple, clear language accessible to non-technical users
- Avoid jargon and technical terms where possible
- Be helpful and reassuring in all messages

## Visual Identity

- **Primary Color:** Blue (#2563EB) - Conveys trust, reliability, professionalism
- **Secondary Color:** Light Blue (#3B82F6) - For accents and highlights
- **Background:** Clean whites and light grays
- **Typography:** Modern, readable sans-serif fonts

## Error Messages

- **Approach:** User-friendly, no technical codes
- Always explain what happened and what user can do
- Examples:
  - ✓ "Your license has expired. Please renew to continue using."
  - ✓ "This device cannot be activated. You've reached your device limit."
  - ✗ "ERR_LICENSE_INVALID_0x003"

## Language Support

- **Primary:** English
- **Secondary:** Vietnamese
- All UI text and messages must be translatable
- Implement i18n from the start

## Brand Voice

- Be helpful, not demanding
- Guide users through problems
- Celebrate successful actions (activation, renewal)
